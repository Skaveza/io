01 · Featured Project

Healthbridge

Remote Healthcare Access Platform

What if distance, danger, or a broken healthcare system couldn't stop someone from getting help? Healthbridge is built for people in remote or unsafe areas who can't reach a clinic. It connects them to medical providers through secure, encrypted messaging in real time. When a patient needs more than advice, they can share their location directly through the app so that physical help can find its way to them. Safe, fast, and private by design.

02 · Infrastructure

Waterpath.ke

Water Access and Community Resilience Platform

Turkana is one of Kenya's driest counties, and its water crisis is both urgent and solvable. Waterpath.ke lets residents locate the nearest safe water points using geospatial querying (Haversine distance), report broken or unsafe sources via SMS — which triggers technician dispatch through Africa's Talking — and view live infrastructure status on an NGO-facing dashboard. An ML endpoint also predicts water quality from geographic features. A small system with a very large purpose.

03 · Financial Inclusion

Uwezo KYC

Mobile Money KYC for the Unbanked

Most KYC systems demand formal bank statements that millions of Africans simply don't have. Uwezo is a proof of concept built around a different question: what if your receipts were enough? A FastAPI backend processes physical receipts through a LayoutLMv3 pipeline (PyTorch) to extract structured financial data, then scores identity using document quality, transaction patterns, and behavioural consistency. The next step is extending this to mobile money transaction history, giving formal recognition to people formal systems have never seen.
